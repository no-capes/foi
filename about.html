    About the restaurant


    Flavours of India was started in 2006 by the entrepreneurial couple, Sukhi and Simran Josan. The couple has worked tirelessly to provide the best service and Indian food experience to our customers. We continue having the honour to serve you all the delicious flavours of India through our vast menu.

    Flavours of India is located in the centre of Regina and whether you want a quick snack or a full meal, Flavours of India has all the options for you with great prices and of course, great food. Our menu caters to every taste palette and various food intolerances. All our dishes can be made to your preferred spice level to give you the most enjoyable experience.

    If you are searching for an authentic and delicious Indian food experience, look no further than Flavours of India. We are here to serve you and make you fall in love with Indian food, as much as us. We look forward to welcoming you.
